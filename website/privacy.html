Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 4, 2025

At Stremax, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our services.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We may collect personal information that you provide, including:

  • Name and email address
  • GitHub username
  • Blockchain wallet addresses
  • Usage data and preferences

Technical Information

We automatically collect certain information when you use our services:

  • IP address and device information
  • Browser type and settings
  • Operating system information
  • Log data and usage statistics

How We Use Your Information

Service Provision

  • Provide and maintain our services
  • Process your transactions
  • Send service notifications
  • Respond to your requests

Improvement and Analysis

  • Analyze usage patterns
  • Improve user experience
  • Debug technical issues
  • Develop new features

Information Sharing

Third-Party Service Providers

We may share information with trusted third parties who assist us in:

  • Hosting and maintaining our services
  • Processing payments
  • Analyzing usage data
  • Customer support

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law or in response to:

  • Court orders
  • Legal process
  • Government requests
  • Protection of rights

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and monitoring
  • Secure data storage practices

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Data portability

To exercise these rights, please contact us at stremaxteam@proton.me

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze usage patterns
  • Improve our services
  • Provide security features

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last updated" date
  • Sending an email notification for significant changes
